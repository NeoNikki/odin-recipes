Egg Salad

This is the best damn egg salad your lil legion of taste buds will ever encounter. Do yourself a favor and just fucking whip this shit up already.

Ingredients
  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 3 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
  • 1 Tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 Tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tsp capers
Instuctions
  1. chop them eggs
  2. chop everything else
  3. chop it all together
