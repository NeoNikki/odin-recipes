Ingredients
  • cooking spray
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped fresh asparagus
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 Tbsp chopped red onion
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions
  1. Chop them veggies
  2. fry them veggies
  3. mix them eggs up with that milk, salt and pepper
  4. add cooked veggies
  5. spray dat pan
  6. pour your egg juice in that pan
  7. bake at 350° for 20 minutes yo
